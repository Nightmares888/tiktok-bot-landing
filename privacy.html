Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled by the Hard Work application.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Hard Work is a local automation script. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, user information, or video content to external servers. All operations are processed strictly on the user's local machine.

2. Third-Party Services

The application interacts directly with the TikTok API to facilitate video uploads. Any authorization credentials (such as Client Keys, Secrets, or Access Tokens) are stored locally on your machine and are never shared, sold, or exposed to third parties.

3. Data Security

Since no data is uploaded to our servers, your data security depends entirely on the security of your local environment.
